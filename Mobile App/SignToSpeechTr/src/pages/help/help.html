SSTST

Sinhala Sign To Speech Translator

What is SSTST ?

Sign language is a visual language use to communicate with people who are suffering from hearing loss and get little or no benefit from technology. Sign language typically involves with hand shapes, facial expression, gestures and body language. This app is capable of recognizing hand gestures involves in Sri Lankan Sign Language.

How it works ?

Application takes a video input, frame the video into thumbnail images, send to backend server and display the output. Image recognition and gesture detection happen at the backend server. Active internet connection is needed for the functionality.

How to use ?

Record a new video

Click on "RECORD VIDEO" button to record a new video. Then device camera will be launched. Accept the video after recording in order to upload it to the server.

Upload a video from the gallery

Click on "OPEN GALLERY" button to select a video from the device storage. After selecting the video, app will automatically upload it to the server.

Translate the video

Click on "TRANSLATE" button to generate the text. This button will be disabled until you select a video.

Play audio

You can play the audio by clicking on red microphone button. This button will be disabled until you translate a video.
